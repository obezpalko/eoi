## üá¨üáß Rules in English

* **Goal:** Avoid being the first to collect **10 "Fool Points."**
* **Setup:** Each player gets a small whiteboard and a **marker** (3 to 11 players).
* **Gameplay:**
	1. Draw a card from the **deck**. A question about school subjects is read aloud.
	2. Every player writes their answer in secret. You aren't looking for the "right" answer, but the "popular" one.
	3. Everyone reveals their boards at once.
* **Scoring:**
	- If your answer **matches** the majority, you are safe.
	- If your answer is the **least common** (unique or very rare), you are **penalized** with a Fool Point.
* **The End:** The first person to reach 10 points is the loser. The winners get one minute to laugh at the "Village Idiot."
